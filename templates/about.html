We are a fully-fledged Race Timing using an urbane state of the art Race Timing Solution utilizing RFID Timing Technology from MYLAPS. Our solution utilizes the MYLAPS BibTag timing system which is suitable for mass events with affordable chips and the highest read-rate in the industry. We use different solutions for different sports and tags are chosen based on the suitability for the sport, size of the event and the setting in which the event is held.

More Services

Trophies

We supply custom made trophies for any event using various materials. The trophies are specifically designed to meet the event requirements with quality in mind.

Route Marking

Our team members are qualified and certified route markers thus we are able mark routes for any event big or small.

Timing Setup

Running chip timing systems combine chips (tags) worn by athletes and detection mats along the course, for example at the start or finish line. The MYLAPS BibTag System has the highest read-rates in the industry.

Race Clocks

We provide various race clocks for use at events on rental basis however we also supply race clocks for purchase. The clocks include tripod, hanging, car mounted, motor bicycle mounted, start and finish line clocks.

Medals

We supply custom made medals for any event. The medals are specifically designed to meet the event requirements with quality in mind.

Race Number Printing

We offer in-house personalized race number printing using digital printing on high-quality color polyester paper. Our race numbers are waterproof and printed on tear proof material. We can print different race number models with own colors.
